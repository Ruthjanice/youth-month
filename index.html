Youth Month

Youth Month is an annual event designed to bring youth access and awareness of opportunities and transformational insight. It seeks to create a one month long event that is the largest and longest in campus empowerment experience in universities in Kenya.

Read More

About Youth Month

Who we are

"Future forward" expresses that the event is designed to help young people prepare for the future, develop the skills and knowledge they need to succeed, and take an active role in shaping their own futures.

The theme of "future forward" inspires young people to think about their goals and aspirations, and to take steps towards achieving them while encouraging them to explore emerging trends and technologies that are likely to shape the future of work and society.

Pillars

Youth Month Pillars

Future Skills Workshops

This includes workshops and sessions that focus on developing the skills that young people will need to succeed in the future, such as digital literacy, creativity, critical thinking, adaptability and their role in the community.

Entrepreneurship and innovation

This includes sessions and activities that encourage young people to think creatively and develop entrepreneurial skills that will make them relevant in an evolving world

Networking and Mentorship

The aim is to provide opportunities for young people to connect with mentors and peers in their fields of interest.

Career Development and Placement

This includes career development resources such as resume workshops, interview coaching, and job placement services as well as provide guidance on emerging career paths.
