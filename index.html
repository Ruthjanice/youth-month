Youth Month

Youth Month is an annual event organized by AIESEC, founded in 2022, where the Country Managers, members and youths to build new projects, network, learn new skills.Celebrated from September to October, This event serves as a platform for young people to come together, share experiences, and discuss critical global issues. It features interactive workshops, inspiring keynote speeches, and insightful panel discussions, creating an environment that fosters personal growth, leadership development, and cross-cultural understanding.

Pillars

Youth Month Pillars

BIG 4 FOCUS AREAS:

Future Skills Workshops
This includes workshops and sessions that focus on developing the skills that young people will need to succeed in the future, such as digital literacy, creativity, critical thinking, adaptability and their role in the community.
Entrepreneurship and innovation
This includes sessions and activities that encourage young people to think creatively and develop entrepreneurial skills that will make them relevant in an evolving world.
Networking and Mentorship
The aim is to provide opportunities for young people to connect with mentors and peers in their fields of interest.
Career Development and Placement
This includes career development resources such as resume workshops, interview coaching, and job placement services as well as provide guidance on emerging career paths.

Last Year's Partners

The Old Mutual Foundation, affiliated with the Old Mutual Group and Faulu Microfinance Bank, serves as our social responsibility division. During youth month 2022, the Foundation collaborated with AIESEC to educate students and young individuals about making improved financial choices. These efforts included promoting the importance of early savings and fostering financial awareness among the youth.

By training the youth in skills related to banking sector and offering internships for our international network, they can access career skills that employers look out for, get professional help from experts on how to draft better CVs and cover letters as well as unlimited access to job opportunities.
